About Us



Hello! My name is Hrithik Chaudhary, and I'm currently a sophomore studying Computer Science at New Jersey Institute of Technology.

I'm a Nepalese International student at NJIT. After finishing my high school, I had planned to get accepted in a highly reputed college and so I chose NJIT over many other colleges. Besides the great value that NJIT has, it being so close to New York was one of the major reasons why I chose to pursue my Bachelors degree here.

You miss 100% of the shots you dont take. Wayne Gretzki-

Michael Scott, The Office Season 5, Episode 21.

I’ve been into programming since a long time and my love for coding only seems to be increasing over time. Even though I’m still a sophomore, I feel like I have learned a lot and my passion for coding has been tremendously met at NJIT. My CS professors have been well over my expectations and have provided me with much useful concepts besides exam content.

After Graduating in 22, I plan on working in New York in a well-reputed company. My high hopes are for Tesla, but its not definite. I would definitely want to pursue my post-Bachelors career as a data scientist as I'm currently enrolled in Data Analytics minor and Data Mining is something I've looked onto for way too long.
